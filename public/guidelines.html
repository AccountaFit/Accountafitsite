ACCOUNTAFIT COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Effective Date: April 27, 2026
AccountaFit Corp | Incorporated in Delaware

OUR MISSION

AccountaFit exists to help people achieve their fitness goals by connecting them with real accountability partners. Our community is built on trust, respect, and commitment. These Community Guidelines define the standards of behavior that every member of the AccountaFit community must uphold.

Violations of these Guidelines may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent removal from the Platform.

1. THIS IS NOT A DATING APP

We want to be completely clear: AccountaFit is a fitness accountability platform, period.

  • Using AccountaFit to pursue romantic or sexual connections is strictly prohibited.
  • Sending flirtatious, romantic, or sexual messages to other users will result in immediate account removal.
  • Attempts to arrange romantic or sexual meetings through the Platform will result in immediate permanent ban.

We take this rule seriously and enforce it strictly. If you are looking for a dating app, AccountaFit is not the place.

2. TREAT EVERY MEMBER WITH RESPECT

Our community thrives when people feel safe, supported, and respected. You must:

  • Treat all users with dignity and respect, regardless of their fitness level, body type, age, gender, race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, religion, or disability.
  • Communicate with your partner in a positive, encouraging, and constructive manner.
  • Accept that people are at different stages of their fitness journey and support them accordingly.

Zero tolerance for:
- Bullying, mocking, or shaming users for their fitness level or body
- Hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language of any kind
- Threats or intimidation of any kind

3. BE HONEST

The accountability system only works when everyone is honest. You must:

  • Provide accurate information about your fitness goals, experience level, and schedule when creating your profile.
  • Honestly report your workouts and check-ins — do not falsify your activity.
  • Be upfront with your partner about your schedule, limitations, and commitments.

Misrepresenting your fitness level or identity to obtain a match is a violation of these Guidelines and our Terms of Service.

4. HONOR YOUR COMMITMENTS

Accountability is the foundation of AccountaFit. When you make a commitment to a partner:

  • Show up. If you said you would check in, check in.
  • Communicate early if your plans change — do not ghost your partner.
  • Treat the shared streak and the partnership with the respect it deserves.
  • If you cannot continue the partnership, communicate that clearly and professionally.

Repeatedly abandoning partners without communication may result in account suspension.

5. PROTECT PERSONAL INFORMATION

Respect your partner's privacy:

  • Do not share another user's personal information (name, photos, location, contact details) with anyone outside the Platform without their explicit consent.
  • Do not screenshot or record video calls with other users without their consent.
  • Do not attempt to identify users outside of the Platform or contact them through other channels without their permission.

6. KEEP COMMUNICATIONS APPROPRIATE

All messages, video calls, and content shared on AccountaFit must be:

  • Focused on fitness, accountability, and goal achievement.
  • Free from sexual, romantic, or explicitly personal content.
  • Free from spam, solicitation, or promotional material.
  • Free from offensive, vulgar, or threatening language.

You may not use the messaging features to:
- Send unsolicited commercial messages or spam
- Solicit money, gifts, or personal favors from other users
- Share inappropriate images or media of any kind

7. CONTENT STANDARDS

Any content you share on the Platform must:

  • Be appropriate for a general fitness audience
  • Not contain nudity, sexually suggestive content, or graphic violence
  • Not promote dangerous, illegal, or harmful activities
  • Not infringe on the intellectual property or privacy rights of others
  • Not include personal contact information (phone numbers, email addresses, social media handles) in your public profile

8. VIDEO CALL CONDUCT

Short video calls may be available to help partners align on goals and expectations. During any video call you must:

  • Remain fully clothed and in an appropriate setting
  • Keep the conversation focused on fitness goals and partnership expectations
  • Not record, screenshot, or share the call without explicit consent from the other user
  • Treat the other user with the same respect you would expect in a professional setting

Any inappropriate conduct during video calls will result in immediate account removal.

9. REPORTING AND ENFORCEMENT

How to report: If you experience or witness a violation of these Guidelines, report it immediately through the in-app reporting feature or by emailing info@accountafit.com. All reports are treated as confidential.

What happens when you report: Our team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action, which may include:

  • Issuing a warning to the offending user
  • Temporarily suspending the offending user's account
  • Permanently banning the offending user from the Platform
  • Reporting the conduct to law enforcement if appropriate

What we cannot do: We cannot guarantee the behavior of other users. We encourage you to use your judgment, report issues promptly, and prioritize your own safety.

10. SAFETY TIPS

  • Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong about an interaction, report it and disengage.
  • Protect your identity. You are not required to share your full name, phone number, home address, or workplace with your accountability partner.
  • If you meet in person (which is entirely your own decision and at your own risk), meet in a public place and tell a trusted friend or family member where you are going.
  • Never send money to another user on the Platform under any circumstances.
  • Block and report anyone who makes you feel unsafe or uncomfortable.

Violations of these Community Guidelines are also violations of our Terms of Service and may result in account termination. AccountaFit Corp reserves the right to update these Guidelines at any time.
