ACCOUNTAFIT SAFETY POLICY

Effective Date: April 27, 2026
AccountaFit Corp | Incorporated in Delaware

OUR COMMITMENT TO YOUR SAFETY

At AccountaFit Corp, your safety is a top priority. While we are a fitness accountability platform — not a dating app — we recognize that connecting strangers online carries real responsibility. This Safety Policy outlines the protections we have in place, the steps you can take to stay safe, and how we handle safety concerns.

1. WHAT ACCOUNTAFIT DOES TO PROTECT YOU

1.1 Profile Review. We reserve the right to review and remove profiles that contain inappropriate content, misrepresent identity, or violate our Terms of Service.

1.2 Reporting System. Every user has the ability to report another user's behavior at any time through the in-app reporting feature or by emailing info@accountafit.com.

1.3 Blocking. Every user can block another user at any time. Blocked users cannot contact you or view your profile.

1.4 Account Removal. We will promptly remove users found to have violated our Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, or Safety Policy, including any user found to be using the Platform for purposes other than fitness accountability.

1.5 Law Enforcement Cooperation. AccountaFit Corp will cooperate fully with law enforcement in any investigation involving potential illegal activity on the Platform, including providing user information as permitted by law and our Privacy Policy.

2. WHAT WE CANNOT GUARANTEE

AccountaFit does not conduct criminal background checks on users. We cannot verify the accuracy of information provided by users or guarantee that all users will behave in accordance with our policies.

The following important limitations apply:

  • AccountaFit cannot prevent all inappropriate behavior on the Platform.
  • AccountaFit is not responsible for the conduct of users online or offline.
  • AccountaFit cannot guarantee the safety of any in-person interactions between users.
  • AccountaFit is not responsible for any harm arising from interactions with other users.

These limitations are the reason we ask every user to exercise personal caution, trust their instincts, and report any concerning behavior immediately.

3. PROHIBITED CONDUCT — ZERO TOLERANCE

The following behaviors will result in immediate permanent account removal with no warning:

  • Using the Platform for romantic, sexual, or dating purposes
  • Sending sexually explicit messages, images, or content to any user
  • Threatening, stalking, or harassing any user on or off the Platform
  • Sharing another user's personal information without their consent (doxing)
  • Soliciting or accepting money from other users
  • Creating fake profiles or impersonating another person
  • Any conduct that violates applicable law

4. PROTECTING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

You are in control of what personal information you share on AccountaFit. We strongly recommend:

  • Do not share your full legal name, home address, workplace, or daily routine with your accountability partner until you have built sufficient trust.
  • Do not share your personal phone number or social media profiles through the Platform.
  • Use the Platform's communication tools rather than moving conversations off-platform prematurely.
  • Do not share financial information with any user, ever.

5. VIDEO CALL SAFETY

If you use AccountaFit's video call feature:

  • You are not required to use your real name or reveal your location.
  • Be in a safe, private location where you feel comfortable.
  • If the other person behaves inappropriately, end the call immediately and report it.
  • Do not share personal identifying information (home address, workplace, vehicle) during calls.
  • Remember that recording calls without consent may be illegal in your jurisdiction.

6. IF YOU CHOOSE TO MEET IN PERSON

AccountaFit does not organize or encourage in-person meetings between users. However, if two accountability partners mutually decide to meet in person, we strongly recommend the following safety precautions:

  • Meet in a public place such as a gym, park, or fitness class — never at a private residence for a first meeting.
  • Tell someone you trust where you are going, who you are meeting, and when you expect to return.
  • Arrange your own transportation — do not accept rides from someone you have just met.
  • Keep your phone charged and have emergency contacts readily accessible.
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, leave immediately.
  • Do not feel pressured to meet anyone in person. There is no obligation to do so.

AccountaFit Corp assumes no liability for any harm, injury, or loss arising from in-person meetings between users. Any decision to meet in person is made entirely at your own risk.

7. SEXUAL MISCONDUCT POLICY

AccountaFit Corp has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward all forms of sexual misconduct. This includes:

  • Sending or requesting sexual images or content
  • Making sexually suggestive comments or advances
  • Engaging in sexual harassment of any kind
  • Non-consensual sharing of intimate images
  • Any sexual contact that is non-consensual

If you experience sexual misconduct by another user:

  1. Remove yourself from the situation if you are in immediate danger — call emergency services (911 in the US) if necessary.
  2. Block the user immediately on the Platform.
  3. Report the user through the in-app report feature or email info@accountafit.com with as much detail as possible.
  4. Preserve any evidence (screenshots, messages) if it is safe to do so.
  5. Consider reporting the conduct to local law enforcement.

AccountaFit Corp will investigate all reports of sexual misconduct and will permanently remove any user found to have engaged in such behavior.

8. EMERGENCY RESOURCES

If you are in immediate danger, call 911 (US) or your local emergency number.

Additional resources:

  • National Sexual Assault Hotline: 1-800-656-4673 | rainn.org
  • National Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-7233 | thehotline.org
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Cyber Civil Rights Initiative (non-consensual image sharing): cybercivilrights.org

9. REPORTING TO ACCOUNTAFIT

To report a safety concern, inappropriate behavior, or policy violation:

  • In-App: Use the report button on any user profile or message
  • Email: info@accountafit.com — please include as much detail as possible
  • Subject Line: Use "SAFETY REPORT" to ensure priority handling

All safety reports are treated as confidential and handled with urgency. We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours and take action as quickly as possible.

10. OUR REVIEW PROCESS

When a safety report is received:

  1. The report is immediately flagged for review by our team.
  2. The reported user's account may be temporarily suspended pending investigation.
  3. We review all available evidence, including messages and account history.
  4. Appropriate action is taken, up to and including permanent account removal.
  5. We will notify you of the outcome to the extent permitted by our Privacy Policy.

11. GOVERNING LAW

This Safety Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

AccountaFit Corp reserves the right to update this Safety Policy at any time. Your safety matters to us — please do not hesitate to reach out.
